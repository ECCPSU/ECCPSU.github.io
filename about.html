 About.

At Engineering - Consulting Collaborative, we combine the technical skill set of both engineering and business students to not only foster the growth of the downtown State College environment through local business triumphs, but also host/participate various competitions which mature the development of problem-solving and cross-discipline teamwork essential for working in today’s corporate environment. Founded in 2016, our goal is to make your vision, our mission: whether its utilizing the technical ability of our talented board to administer constructive events or delivering unparalleled solutions to our clients, we, at E-CC, ensure to give our best. Our specialty is organizing case-competitions every semester to ignite personal, intellectual and professional development among members of the university.
