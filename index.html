Who We Are

Engineering - Consulting Collaborative is a club centered around business and engineering. We strive to participate, attend and host consulting case competitions, design tournaments, and other events that promote creativity.
For great work to happen, Collaboration needs to happen first

- Kartik Singhal, Founder / Advisor

INITIATIVES

Case Competitions

We participate in case competitions that provide members the opportunity to learn about pressing problems businesses face. These competitions allow our members to think analytically and work in a dynamic team environment.
​​
​

Local Business Consulting

​Our team is focused on providing local businesses with assistance on a wide variety of projects. We are qualified and enthusiastic to help with marketing plans, website creation, social media presence, brand enhancement, or any other challenges they might be facing in the ever-changing business landscape.

Training for Case Competitions

E-CC conducts training sessions for members to equip them with the skills required to excel in case competitions. We also host our own case competitions throughout the semester to give students a better understanding of the problems faced by companies in the industry. 

Events

At E-CC, we aim to bring together members and companies in an effort to promote the core consulting career on campus. All students regardless of major are welcome to attend our company sponsored information sessions, networking conferences and competitions.
